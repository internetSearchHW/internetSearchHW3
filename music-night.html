Italian Edition

International Music Night

Event information

We invite you to join us in next event of our international Music Nights series – this time exploring Italy and its popular culture in regards to its beautiful melodies. Starting from such iconic masterpieces of 20th century as Felicita L'italiano right up to Grande Amore and Soldi.

As usual, we will have our residental live band "Young Travellers" for the first part of the night and DJ Maxx will help to keep our energy up in the other half of the night.

Non less important – we will have guests to help to share stories about the magnificent sounds of Italy and how it has shaped their life.

Also we will have special offer for Italian cuisine foods and drinks!

See you there!
